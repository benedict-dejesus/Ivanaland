# IVANALAND — Release Notes

## v1.0.0 — "The Crown Jewel"

The first public release of IVANALAND, by **Benedict de Jesus**.

### Highlights
- One seamless handcrafted world (4000×5800) with ten living districts:
  Creator Studio City, Family Village, Foodie Market, Beauty Boulevard,
  Pet Paradise Park, Travel Terminal, Luxury Resort, Fan Festival Plaza,
  Charity Corner, and Adventure Island.
- **100 lost iPhones**, each with its own name, hiding story, hint, difficulty
  tier, and discovery method — including four legendary multi-step discoveries
  and the locked signature finale: **Phone #100, inside the Giant Festival
  Statue's crown.**
- Player name entry at first launch; a personalized **Certificate of
  Completion** (PNG export / native share) when all 100 are recovered.
- Live **iPhones Found counter** that opens the full 1–100 checklist with
  per-phone hints and found-phone camera jumps.
- Rewarding discovery celebrations: starburst rays, shockwave rings, confetti
  fountains, rising-phone hero animation, procedural fanfares.
- 24 achievements · milestone banners at 25/50/75/99 · progress share cards.
- Automatic LocalStorage saves; corruption-safe; no backend, no accounts.
- 60 FPS-targeted rendering: per-district texture caching, view culling,
  pooled particles, capped device-pixel ratio.
- Accessibility: reduced-motion support, ARIA-labelled UI, ≥44 px targets,
  unlimited free hints.

### Known notes
- Desktop is fully supported (drag + scroll-zoom) but the experience is tuned
  portrait-first for mobile.
- Sound is procedural WebAudio and starts after the first interaction
  (mobile autoplay policy).
