# 🗺️ IVANALAND — Complete Walkthrough

**⚠️ FULL SPOILERS — all 100 lost iPhones.**
For players who want every last one. By Benedict de Jesus.

## How discoveries work

- **Tap** — the phone itself is somewhere in view (plain, camouflaged, or peeking
  out). Tap it directly.
- **Container** — tap the host object once to open it, then tap the phone inside
  (it closes again after ~6 seconds, but reopens freely).
- **Reveal** — tap the *trigger* object; the phone pops out beside it and stays
  revealed. Then tap the phone.
- **Sequence** — tap the numbered steps in order (the current step always
  shimmers softly). Wrong taps never reset your progress. The final step reveals
  the phone.
- Every unfound phone **glints cyan** every 10–20 seconds — if you're stuck,
  stop panning and watch. (Settings → "Extra glints" makes this more frequent;
  at 95+ found, glints double automatically.)
- Hints are free and unlimited in the checklist (📱 counter → 💡). Viewing a
  hint twice also shows the phone's district and landmark.
