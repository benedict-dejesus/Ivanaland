# IVANALAND — 05 DISTRICT BIBLE

Ten districts. Each entry: narrative, landmarks, props, NPCs, interactions, hiding
opportunities. Phones per district: 10 (see 09_PHONE_BIBLE for all 100).

---

## 1. Creator Studio City (hub — sunshine accent)

**Narrative:** The beating heart of Ivanaland — a bustling creator campus where
today's vlog is always being filmed. Cameras roll, ring lights glow, a giant
"IVANALAND" marquee crowns the soundstage.

- **Landmarks:** Grand Soundstage with marquee; Vlog Tower (screen playing "LIVE");
  Studio Roundabout fountain; Editing Café; rooftop billboard.
- **Props:** camera cranes, ring lights, clapperboards, tripods, prop crates,
  golden play-button trophies, coffee cups, cable reels, green-screen wall.
- **NPCs:** camera operator panning; director with megaphone; intern sprinting with
  coffee; mascot in a sun costume waving; drone camera orbiting the tower.
- **Interactions:** tap marquee → bulbs chase; tap clapperboard → "ACTION!" snap;
  tap green screen → cycles beach/space/mountain backdrops; fountain splashes.
- **Hiding opportunities:** inside prop crates, behind the green screen (revealed on
  backdrop swap), atop the marquee letters, in the trophy cabinet, in the café's
  pastry case, under the director's chair.

## 2. Family Village (cozy — grass-light accent)

**Narrative:** Warm homes with laundry lines and lechon Sundays; three generations
share one long merienda table down the main lane.

- **Landmarks:** Grand Family House with wraparound porch; village plaza with
  mango tree; sari-sari corner store; basketball half-court; koi pond.
- **Props:** laundry lines, tricycles, potted orchids, bikes, garden gnomes,
  grills, mailboxes, swing set, water tower.
- **NPCs:** lola rocking on the porch; kids playing tag around the mango tree;
  dad grilling; toddler chasing a rooster; cat sleeping on a warm roof.
- **Interactions:** tap laundry → sheets flap; tap grill → smoke puff & sizzle;
  tap basketball → a shot arcs and (sometimes) rims out; koi scatter on tap.
- **Hiding opportunities:** in a hanging shirt's pocket, mailbox, mango-tree
  canopy, under the porch steps, inside the water-tower hatch, koi-pond glimmer.

## 3. Foodie Market (coral accent)

**Narrative:** A fiesta of stalls under banderitas — sizzling, steaming, and one
legendary chili challenge that nobody has finished.

- **Landmarks:** Grand Market Hall arch; halo-halo tower stand; giant lechon
  float; chili-challenge stage; fruit pyramid stall.
- **Props:** crates of mangoes, steaming pots, woks with fire, hanging sausages,
  parasols, weighing scales, taho carts, ice blocks.
- **NPCs:** vendor tossing a pancake; taho vendor calling out; seagull eyeing
  fish stall; customer fanning a chili-burned mouth; kid balancing ice cream.
- **Interactions:** tap pots → lids rattle & steam; tap fruit pyramid → wobble
  (never falls); tap chili stage → contestant turns red; banderitas ripple.
- **Hiding opportunities:** inside a steaming pot (lift lid), within the fruit
  pyramid, in the lechon float's apple, under crate lids, halo-halo glass glint.

## 4. Beauty Boulevard (berry accent)

**Narrative:** A glamorous promenade of salons and mirror-bright storefronts where
everyone's ready for their close-up — even the mannequins.

- **Landmarks:** Mirror Salon with heart-shaped mirror; Runway Walk crosswalk;
  Perfume Fountain; giant lipstick sculpture; Vanity Rooftop sign.
- **Props:** makeup counters, hair-dryer chairs, mirrors, perfume bottles,
  shopping bags, velvet ropes, mannequins, string lights.
- **NPCs:** stylist snipping air; mannequin that *winks*; influencer filming a
  transition; makeup artist with a giant brush; poodle with a fresh blowout.
- **Interactions:** tap mirrors → light-sweep flash; tap perfume fountain →
  sparkle mist; tap dryer chair → hair inflates; tap runway → strut & camera
  flashes.
- **Hiding opportunities:** phone as a **reflection** first (mirror shows it, real
  one is nearby), inside a shopping bag, in the lipstick cap, mannequin's clutch
  purse, under a salon chair, behind the rooftop sign letters.

## 5. Pet Paradise Park (grass accent)

**Narrative:** A park run *by* the pets, honestly — agility courses, a bone-shaped
pool, and a corgi mayor with an office (a doghouse with a nameplate).

- **Landmarks:** bone-shaped pool; agility course; Corgi Mayor's doghouse; cat
  tower pagoda; duck pond with fountain.
- **Props:** balls, frisbees, chew toys, food bowls, leashes, pet-groomer tent,
  scratching posts, birdhouses.
- **NPCs:** corgi patrolling importantly; cat ignoring everyone from the pagoda;
  dog walker tangled in six leashes; parrot repeating "hello!"; ducks in a line.
- **Interactions:** tap corgi → happy spin; tap pool → cannonball splash by a
  golden retriever; tap parrot → speech bubble; tap ball → it bounces away.
- **Hiding opportunities:** buried mound that a dog digs up on tap, inside the
  doghouse (corgi steps aside), cat-tower top, duck-pond lily pad, kibble bowl.

## 6. Travel Terminal (sky accent)

**Narrative:** The gateway to Ivanaland — part airport, part jeepney terminal,
all wanderlust. Departure board lists destinations like "ANYWHERE FUN".

- **Landmarks:** control tower with radar; departure hall with split-flap board;
  luggage carousel; jeepney bay; hot-air balloon pad.
- **Props:** suitcases (every color), luggage carts, boarding passes, cones,
  windsocks, a small plane, escalators, potted palms.
- **NPCs:** pilot waving from cockpit; traveler chasing a rolling suitcase;
  ground crew with glowing wands; sniffer beagle inspecting bags; balloon pilot.
- **Interactions:** tap departure board → flaps shuffle to a joke destination;
  tap carousel → starts/stops; tap windsock → gust; tap balloon → flame whoosh.
- **Hiding opportunities:** inside a specific polka-dot suitcase on the moving
  carousel (pauses at the chute), radar dish cradle, jeepney glovebox, x-ray
  tray, balloon basket, escalator handrail slot.

## 7. Luxury Resort (sand/water accent)

**Narrative:** Infinity pools, swaying palms, and a beach wedding perpetually five
minutes from the kiss. Paradise, professionally maintained.

- **Landmarks:** Grand Resort Hotel; infinity pool; beach wedding arch; overwater
  cabanas; lighthouse point.
- **Props:** sun loungers, umbrellas, cocktail glasses, towels swans, surfboards,
  tiki torches, room-service carts, yachts.
- **NPCs:** lifeguard scanning; newlyweds posing; masseuse mid-chop; kid building
  a sandcastle; waiter balancing a tray across the pool deck.
- **Interactions:** tap palms → sway + falling coconut (bonks nothing); tap
  pool → swimmer does a lap; tap wedding arch → petal burst; tiki torches flare.
- **Hiding opportunities:** inside a towel swan, sandcastle turret, cocktail with
  a glowing "ice cube", cabana ceiling fan, lighthouse lamp room, yacht cabin.

## 8. Fan Festival Plaza (berry/sunshine — signature district)

**Narrative:** The community's thank-you party that never ends: stages, fan-art
walls, light-stick oceans — all watched over by the **Giant Festival Statue**
holding a torch of confetti.

- **Landmarks:** **Giant Festival Statue** (tallest structure; crown holds Phone
  #100); main stage; fan-art wall; ferris wheel; confetti cannon battery.
- **Props:** light sticks, banners, balloon arches, merch stands, speaker stacks,
  photo booths, food trucks, lantern strings.
- **NPCs:** crowd wave sections; hype MC; fan painting the art wall; security
  guard nodding to the beat; lantern vendor.
- **Interactions:** tap stage → light show; tap confetti cannons → BOOM confetti;
  tap ferris wheel → speeds up briefly; tap crowd → the wave; statue's torch
  shimmers when tapped (subtle hint escalation near completion).
- **Hiding opportunities:** photo-booth prop shelf, speaker-stack grill, ferris
  gondola #7, balloon-arch knot, merch-stand cap rack, **statue crown (Phone
  #100 — legendary, revealed via the statue sequence)**.

## 9. Charity Corner (mint accent)

**Narrative:** The quiet, glowing heart of Ivanaland — a giving square with a
donation carousel, community pantry, and a wishing well that actually grants
wishes (mostly for dogs).

- **Landmarks:** Wishing Well; community pantry shelves; donation-box carousel;
  gift-wrapping pavilion; blood-drive bus (friendly, cheery).
- **Props:** gift boxes, care packages, ribbon spools, canned-goods pyramids,
  teddy bears, thank-you letters wall, balloons.
- **NPCs:** volunteers passing boxes in a chain; kid donating a piggy bank;
  grandma knitting scarves; delivery rider loading care kits.
- **Interactions:** tap well → coin plink + sparkle wish; tap gift boxes → lids
  pop with ribbons; tap teddy shelf → one waves; volunteers high-five on tap.
- **Hiding opportunities:** inside a wrapped gift (one specific ribbon color),
  wishing-well bucket, care-package #47, teddy bear's backpack, ribbon-spool
  core, pantry's top shelf behind cans.

## 10. Adventure Island (jungle greens)

**Narrative:** A jungle playground across a rope bridge — ziplines, a waterfall
with a secret, and a very old shipwreck that the parrots have gentrified.

- **Landmarks:** rope bridge; waterfall with cave behind; zipline towers;
  shipwreck cove; volcano lookout (dormant, occasionally burps smoke rings).
- **Props:** vines, tiki masks, treasure chests, kayaks, campfires, rope coils,
  binocular stands, bananas, glowing mushrooms.
- **NPCs:** zipliner whooping across; monkey stealing a banana; explorer reading
  a map upside down; parrot flock; crab conga line on the beach.
- **Interactions:** tap waterfall → parts briefly (reveals cave glint); tap
  volcano → smoke ring; tap chest → creaks open; tap monkey → banana juggle;
  tap crabs → conga speeds up.
- **Hiding opportunities:** behind the waterfall (tap to part water), treasure
  chest false bottom, zipline pulley bag, shipwreck crow's nest, tiki mask
  mouth, glowing-mushroom cluster (camouflage: phone glow mimics mushrooms).
