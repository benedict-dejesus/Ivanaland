# IVANALAND — 02 CREATIVE DIRECTION

## 1. Visual Identity

**"A living theme-park map painted in candy daylight."**

- Style: modern flat-vector cartoon with soft rounded shapes, gentle gradients, and
  warm drop shadows. Think Disney park map × contemporary casual mobile game.
- Everything is chunky, friendly, and slightly oversized. No sharp corners on
  buildings; roofs bulge, trees are lollipops, waves are scallops.
- Depth via layering + shadow, not perspective: a 3/4 top-down "storybook map" view.
- Absolutely forbidden: pixel art, monochrome, grime, photorealism, retro CRT looks.

## 2. Color Language

Master palette (hex):

| Role | Color | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Sky/water deep | `#2E86DE` | seas, rivers |
| Water shallow | `#6BC5F2` | shorelines, pools |
| Grass base | `#8CD867` | parks, village |
| Grass light | `#A9E27E` | highlights, meadows |
| Sand | `#F7E2A9` | beaches, paths |
| Road | `#B0A8B9` → `#8D8598` | streets, plazas |
| Sunshine accent | `#FFC93C` | landmarks, gold trims |
| Coral accent | `#FF6B6B` | market awnings, flags |
| Berry accent | `#B565D8` | beauty district, festival |
| Mint accent | `#3DDCB1` | charity, health, freshness |
| Cream UI | `#FFF8EC` | panels, cards |
| Ink | `#3A3242` | outlines-lite, text |
| iPhone signal | `#5AC8FA` glow | every phone glints this cyan |

District tinting: each district keeps the master palette but leans on one accent so
players always know where they are (Studio = sunshine, Beauty = berry, Market =
coral, Charity = mint, Resort = sand+water, etc.).

## 3. Tone & Humor Style

- **Wholesome, generous, playful.** The world is kind. Nobody is mocked.
- Humor = visual sight gags and tiny narratives: a dog walking a human, a seagull
  running a snack stand, a mannequin waving back, a drone tangled in a palm tree.
- Filipino warmth woven in respectfully: jeepney-style shuttle, halo-halo stand,
  fiesta banderitas, sari-sari corner store — celebratory, never stereotyping.
- Copywriting voice: short, warm, lightly funny. Phone stories read like one-breath
  anecdotes. Hints are riddle-lite, never smug.

## 4. Inspiration References

- Gameplay: Hidden Folks (interaction density only — NOT its art), Where's Waldo,
  Hidden Through Time.
- Visual: Hidden Through Time, Disney theme-park maps, Cozy Grove's warmth, modern
  casual-game UI (rounded cards, juicy buttons, confetti).
- UX: TikTok-native gestures (tap, pinch, swipe), Facebook-shareable moments.

## 5. Motion Language

- Everything idles: bobs, sways, blinks, drifts. Amplitude small (2–6 px), periods
  desynchronized so the world shimmers rather than pulses.
- Feedback motion is springy: found-phone pop = scale 0→1.2→1 with confetti burst.
- Nothing moves fast enough to be hard to tap; moving phone hosts pause at stations.

## 6. Sound Direction (lightweight)

- Procedural WebAudio chimes only (no audio files): soft "plink" on tap, warm
  arpeggio on discovery, fanfare on milestones, all mutable. Silence-first: game is
  fully playable muted (mobile reality).

## 7. Forbidden Content

- No real photographs or likeness of any real person, including Ivana Alawi. The
  game is *inspired by an event*, not a depiction of a person.
- No begging framing ("give me an iPhone"), no raffle/lottery mechanics, no gambling.
- No ads, no dark patterns, no fake urgency, no data collection.
- No violence, no innuendo, no brand logos (the "iPhone" is a generic glowing
  smartphone silhouette; Apple branding never appears).
- No text that promises a real prize. A disclaimer appears in About: fan-made,
  non-commercial, not affiliated with Ivana Alawi or Apple.
