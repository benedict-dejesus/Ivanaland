# PROJECT CONTEXT

This project is inspired by a real viral social media trend in the Philippines.

Ivana Alawi is one of the Philippines' most popular content creators, actresses, entrepreneurs, influencers, and online personalities.

She is known for:

* Family-centered content
* Lifestyle content
* Travel
* Food adventures
* Humor
* Generosity
* Community engagement
* Large-scale giveaways

Recently, Ivana announced a giveaway involving 100 iPhones.

The giveaway became a viral phenomenon in the Philippines.

Thousands of people created content attempting to gain her attention.

Examples included:

* Fan art
* Tribute videos
* Skits
* Challenges
* Elaborate social media posts
* Personal stories

This project intentionally takes a different approach.

Instead of asking for an iPhone, Benedict de Jesus created an entire game inspired by the event.

The game should feel like:

"A creative gift for the community."

NOT:

"Please give me an iPhone."

The game should remain enjoyable even for players who know nothing about Ivana Alawi.

The game should celebrate creativity, exploration, humor, curiosity, and community.
